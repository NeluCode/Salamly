Welcome to Salamly. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

1. Introduction

Salamly is an Islamic mobile application providing prayer times, Qibla direction, Quran recitation, duas, and Islamic reminders. By using our app, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

A. Automatically Collected Data

  • Device Information: Operating system, app version, device model.
  • Usage Data: Features accessed, interactions, and session durations.

B. User-Provided Data

  • Profile Information: Name, email (if applicable).
  • Preferences: Prayer time settings, Quran translations, notification choices.

C. Location Data

We use GPS data to provide accurate prayer times and Qibla direction. This data is not stored or shared.

D. Internet Usage

The app requires an internet connection to fetch real-time prayer times, duas, and Islamic updates.

E. Push Notifications

We send prayer reminders, duas, and event alerts. You can enable/disable notifications in the app settings.

3. How We Use Your Data

We collect data to:

  • Provide accurate prayer times and Qibla direction.
  • Send Islamic reminders, duas, and alerts.
  • Improve app performance and user experience.
  • Ensure security and prevent unauthorized access.

We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

Salamly integrates with third-party services:

  • Google Play Services: App analytics, crash reports.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: Secure push notifications.
  • Geolocation APIs: Accurate Qibla direction and prayer times.

5. Security Measures

We ensure strong security through:

  • Data encryption for secure storage and transmission.
  • Limited access to sensitive information.
  • Regular updates to protect against vulnerabilities.

6. User Control & Choices

You can:

  • Enable/disable location services.
  • Manage notification settings.
  • Request data deletion by contacting us.

7. Children's Privacy

Salamly is suitable for all ages, but users under 13 years old should use it with parental guidance.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of the app means you accept any changes.
